How It Works

Our process is designed to simplify the complexities of financial management, empowering businesses and individuals to take control of their finances with ease. If you have any questions, we are always just a call or email away.

How Online Bookkeeping Services Work

1

Secure Onboarding

  • Client grants access to cloud accounting software and bank feeds.
  • Bookkeeper reviews your chart of accounts, customizes categories, and imports opening balances.
  • Two-factor authentication and encrypted channels ensure data privacy.
2

Transaction Processing

  • Daily or weekly uploads of receipts, invoices, and bills into the portal.
  • Automated bank and credit-card reconciliations match transactions to entries.
  • Manual review flags anomalies: bookkeeper adjusts and categorizes each item.
3

Client Review & Collaboration

  • Draft financials are delivered each month via a secure dashboard.
  • Client checks for accuracy, asks questions, and requests reclassifications if needed.
  • Changes are implemented before final closing.
4

Reporting & Insights

  • Finalized profit-and-loss statements, balance sheets, and cash-flow reports are published.
  • Variance analyses highlight budget overruns or under-performance.
  • Year-end close packages and sales-tax filings can be generated on demand.

How Online Payroll Services Work start

1

Employee Setup

  • Employer uploads employee data: names, addresses, tax withholdings, and benefit elections.
  • Employers can email the employee data and our experienced staff will take care of all requirements.
  • Direct-deposit details and pay schedules (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly) are configured.
2

Time & Earnings Collection

  • Hours reported via integrated time-tracking app or uploaded timesheets.
  • Automated overtime and differential pay calculations reduce manual errors.
3

Payroll Run & Approval

  • System calculates gross-to-net pay, including tax withholdings and benefit deductions.
  • Draft payroll report is sent to employer for review and electronic sign-off.
4

Payment & Filings

  • Funds are disbursed via ACH or printed checks; pay stubs are posted online.
  • Federal, state, and local payroll taxes are filed and remitted automatically.
  • Garnishments, retirement contributions, and year-end forms (W-2, 1099) are handled seamlessly.
